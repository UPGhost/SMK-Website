Welcome to the Super Mario Kart Tutorial Website

What this website does is teach you how to play the game Super Mario Kart (SMK) for the SNES.

Who am I?

My name is Conor Wood, AKA Templar or Shadow SMK. I'm currently 7th in the world at SMK and have been top 10 for 6 years of the 8+ years I've been competiting. I'm hoping to share my knowledge of the game to bring a new generation of players to the game and raise the level of competition for newcomers and veterans alike. Enjoy browsing the site and hopefully I'll see you at the World Championships! - Templar
